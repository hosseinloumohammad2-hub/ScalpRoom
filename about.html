Our story

Fxscalproom was founded in 2024 by professional forex traders who were frustrated with the state of the trading signal industry. Most signal services rely on aggressive marketing, fake screenshots, and hidden fees. We wanted to build something different — a transparent, accessible platform with verifiable results.

The name "Fxscalproom" comes from "FX Scalp Room" — a reference to the live, fast-paced trading environment we wanted to create. From day one, our philosophy has been simple: discipline over prediction. Instead of trying to forecast every market move, we wait for high-probability setups and execute with precision.

What we do

Fxscalproom delivers four core services to traders worldwide:

1. Live Trading Signals

We publish 3 to 7 high-quality forex and trading signals per week. Each signal includes the trading pair, direction (BUY/SELL), precise entry, stop loss, and three take-profit targets. Signals are categorized as either short-term scalping or long-term position trades.

2. Daily Market Analysis

Every weekday at 12:00 UTC+3, our team publishes daily analysis covering 20 forex pairs, 5 cryptocurrency markets, and 2 Iran market symbols. Each analysis combines technical chart reading with fundamental context.

3. Long-term Strategic Analysis

Updated weekly, our long-term analysis covers all major asset classes including Forex, Crypto, Indices, Gold, and the Iran Market. This is designed for swing and position traders who need macro context.

4. Economic News Alerts

Automated push notifications five minutes before high-impact economic releases (NFP, FOMC, CPI, ECB, BOE, BOJ). This helps traders avoid getting caught off-guard by sudden volatility.

Technology

Fxscalproom is built as a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means it runs on every modern device without requiring App Store or Play Store downloads. The frontend is a single-page React application with multi-language support. The backend uses Supabase for database, authentication, and real-time updates. Push notifications are delivered through the Web Push protocol with VAPID authentication.

This architecture lets us deliver native-app-quality experiences across iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, and Linux without the friction of App Store approval processes. Updates are deployed instantly. Storage footprint is minimal. Installation takes 5 seconds.
